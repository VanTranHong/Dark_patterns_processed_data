  • Protecting Communities from PFAS in Drinking Water

    Learn more about the proposed regulation

  • Reducing Water Pollution from Power Plants

    EPA is proposing to strengthen the wastewater discharge standards that apply to coal-fired power plants, reducing pollutants discharged through wastewater from coal-fired power plants by approximately 584 million pounds per year. 

    Learn how this proposed rule helps protect our nation’s vital water resources

  • EPA Orders Norfolk Southern to Conduct Cleanup from East Palestine Train Derailment

    EPA ordered Norfolk Southern to conduct all cleanup actions associated with the East Palestine, Ohio train derailment to ensure the health and safety of the community.

    Read more about our response efforts

  • Supporting Pollution Prevention in Underserved Communities

    EPA is making $16 million in Bipartisan Infrastructure Law grant funding available to help businesses adopt pollution prevention practices, advancing environmental justice in communities overburdened by pollution.
