VENTA DE INFORMACION PERSONAL
Solo para residentes de California.

Bajo la Ley de Privacidad del Consumidor de California, usted tiene derecho a optar por no vender su información personal a terceros. Estas cookies recopilan información para análisis y para personalizar su experiencia con anuncios dirigidos. Puede ejercer su derecho a optar por no participar en la venta de información personal.

Si opta por no participar, no podremos ofrecerle anuncios personalizados y no le entregaremos su información personal a terceros. Además, puede comunicarse con nuestro Equipo de privacidad para obtener más aclaraciones sobre sus derechos como consumidor de California visitando nuestra Política de privacidad.

SALE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION
For California Residents Only.

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, you have the right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information to third parties. These cookies collect information for analytics and to personalize your experience with targeted ads. You may exercise your right to opt out of the sale of personal information. If you opt out we will not be able to offer you personalized ads and will not hand over your personal information to any third parties. Additionally, you may contact our Privacy Team for further clarification about your rights as a California consumer by visiting our Privacy Policy.

To OPT-OUT OF SALE, CLICK THE BUTTON.

If you have enabled privacy controls on your browser (such as a plugin), to the extent that it is technically feasible, we strive to accept the browser signal as a valid request to opt-out. Therefore we would not be able to track your activity through the web. This may affect our ability to personalize ads according to your preferences.
