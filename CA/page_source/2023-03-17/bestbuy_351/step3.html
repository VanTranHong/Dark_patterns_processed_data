"Do Not Share" for California Residents

A Do Not Share request will cause us to take actions designed to stop cross-context behavioral advertising to you (which in Best Buy's context means attempting to place advertisements intended for you on others' digital properties). Please look for the “Do Not Sell Or Share My Personal Information” options box on this page, select your state of residence, click the blue “Do Not Sell Or Share My Personal Information” button, and provide the information requested. We also will honor a Do Not Sell request from this website when you do so (more information on Do Not Sell follows). For our mobile app, go to My Account, click on My App Settings and locate the Privacy section. It may take us up to 15 days to process your request here or in our app.

We do not require that visitors log in to their BestBuy.com account to place a request online or in the app, but please note that we can best honor your request if you are logged into your account. If you do not log in when submitting your request, we may not be able to honor your request in the future or on different browsers or devices. If you choose not to log in, or don't have a BestBuy.com account, to best ensure we can honor your request we recommend submitting it again if you visit our site with a different browser or device or if you clear cookies on this browser.

Please note that you may still see advertisements from Best Buy when you are online. For example, let's say you enter “awesome consumer electronics” when doing an online search. We may have asked your search-engine provider to show a Best Buy ad to anyone who makes such a search. You'll see such an ad from Best Buy, but it's only based on your search terms and has nothing to do with any information about you we may have shared with that search-engine provider.

"Do Not Sell"

Best Buy does not sell (as “sell” is traditionally defined) your personal information. That is, we don't provide your name, phone number, address, email address or other personally identifiable information to third parties in exchange for money. But under California law, certain sharing of information for advertising purposes may be considered a “sale” of “personal information.” If you've visited our digital properties within the past 12 months and you've seen ads, under California law personal information about you may have been “sold” to our advertising partners for their own use.

How to opt-out of the Sale of Information Related to You

For our website, click on the “Do Not Sell Or Share My Personal Information” or “Do Not Sell My Personal Information” button on the left side of this page. For our mobile app, go to My Account, click on My App Settings and locate the Privacy section.

What will happen after I select a Do Not Sell option?

After you click the “Do Not Sell Or Share My Personal Information” button or the “Do Not Sell My Personal Information” button on this website:

  • Opt-out cookie: An opt-out cookie will be placed and stored on your browser, for Do Not Sell purposes, preventing personal information from being made available from this website to advertising partners for their own use, independent of Best Buy.
  • Only this browser on this device: The opt-out cookie only applies to the browser you were using and only for the device you were using at the time you made the selection. If you access Best Buy websites from other browsers or devices, you will also need to make this selection on each browser and device.

When you activate the Do Not Sell function of our mobile app, the app will no longer make personal information available to our advertising partners for their own use, independent of Best Buy. It's also possible that portions of the app may not function as intended. If you delete the app, you will need to adjust your settings to opt-out if you ever reinstall the app.

A few things to keep in mind regarding Do Not Sell.
  • Ads and interest-based advertising: You will still see ads. You are not opted out of interest-based advertising. To opt-out of interest-based advertising, visit www.aboutads.info/choices.
  • If you delete or clear cookies, you'll need to click the button again the next time you visit to re-select Do Not Sell. If you delete or clear your cookies, that will delete our opt-out cookie and you will need to opt-out again.
  • Your Do Not Sell selection won't cross from the website to mobile application (or the other way round): If you click the “Do Not Sell Or Share My Personal Information” or “Do Not Sell My Personal Information” on this website, your Do Not Sell selection will not transfer to our mobile app. Similarly, your activation of the Do Not Sell feature on the mobile app won't apply to bestbuy.com. You'll need to do both.
Why don't you take my name and contact information instead for Do Not Sell?
  • We don't ask for your personally identifiable information because we don't need it to honor your Do Not Sell request. A general rule of privacy is to not collect personally identifiable information when you don't need to—so we've set up this method instead.
  • We may not know the information we share with advertising partners is related to you. For example, we may capture and share an identifier or IP address of a device you are using to visit our website, but not have tied that information to you. With this method, we are better ensuring that we honor the intent of your Do Not Sell request, versus just taking your name and address.
What about Global Privacy Control?

If you have enabled Global Privacy Control, we will recognize the GPC signal as a Do Not Sell request. We will treat that request in the same manner as if you hit the “Do Not Sell My Personal Information” button on this website. If you also wish to request that we not share personal information about you for cross-context behavioral advertising, please go to the options box on the left of this page, select your state of residence, hit the blue “Do Not Sell Or Share My Personal Information” button, and provide the information requested.
