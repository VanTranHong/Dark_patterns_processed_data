Keyword Research

Whether your business needs affordable local SEO or national SEO services, Manta will help you find the best keyword opportunities necessary for your business. Our 4-stage testing process identifies high-intent, low-competition keywords that match up with your business offerings, and what people are actually searching for.

Business Directory Listings

Help your business develop authority and increase local rankings by getting listed in relevant online directories. Manta's online business listings management puts your company in the major data aggregators and local directories to enhance brand awareness and increase customer reach with your local SEO search campaign.

Onsite SEO Optimization

Onsite SEO optimization recommendations help enhance keyword positioning and the performance of target pages through SEO strategies implemented on your website. Based on what your business website needs, this can include optimizing descriptions, image alt text, meta information, and much more for recommended keywords.

Offsite Optimization

Offsite optimization focuses heavily on building a website's authority and trust through digital marketing strategies done on other websites on the internet. This can include business listing development, article engagement, link building, and so much more. These offsite tasks help your site increase rankings for recommended keywords over time on search engines.

Content Creation

Manta's team of results-driven online marketing experts create accurate, fresh, and valuable content through website pages and blog articles. It helps businesses target recommended keywords and deliver up-to-date information. All of this works to improve website rankings and helps businesses get found by target customers that can convert into actual sales.

Local Search Optimization

As a local business, there's a ton of opportunities for those in your community to find your business. Manta will help position your website for better local search queries and across directories where your customers search. This includes more location-specific and “near me” keyword strategies. Local SEO is a must if you serve a certain area.
