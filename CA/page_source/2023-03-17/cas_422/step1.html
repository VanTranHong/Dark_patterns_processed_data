    CAS DATA

    Curated scientific knowledge to power discovery.

    High-quality data is the lifeblood of today’s digital R&D pipeline. Every day, our scientists curate, connect, and analyze the valuable data disclosed in scientific publications from around the world to build the CAS Content Collection, covering over 150 years of discoveries.

    Our data underpins all CAS Solutions and can be licensed for machine learning or in-house workflow integration. Our scientists also support custom data curation projects through CAS Custom Services.
