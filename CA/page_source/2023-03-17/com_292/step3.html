Why Bookoo?

Because it's fun! Bookoo is made by yard sale fans, for yard sale fans.

We started Bookoo so we could find the bargains we needed with less running around town and leaving more time for family. What it has become is a better way to buy and sell stuff with neighbors.

For your family

The kids are outgrowing clothes, the new stuff costs a fortune, and the stuff they've outgrown is filling up the garage.

Moms and Dads use Bookoo to:
Sell it for cash.
Save on stuff you need.
Clean up the house.
Clothe kids on a dime.
Get your closet back.

How Does It Work?

Simply contact the seller, meet up, exchange stuff for money and be on your way.

Bookoo brings neighbors together. There's no need for shipping or online transactions between buyers and sellers.
