    Virus of the Day

    pAAV-S5E2-Gq-P2A-dTomato (A

    Depositor: Jordane Dimidschstein

    Purpose: Ready-to-use AAV PHP.eB particles produced from pAAV-S5E2-Gq-P2A-dTomato (#135635). Expression of Gq-DREADD and dTomato (physically separate) under the control of the E2 regulatory element. These AAV were produced with the PHPeB serotype, which permits efficient transduction of the central nervous system. These AAV preparations are suitable purity for injection into animals.
