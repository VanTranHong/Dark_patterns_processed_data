RetailMeNot Privacy Portal

Under the GDPR, the UK GDPR, the CCPA, the CDPA and other privacy legislation, individuals whose personal data is being processed have certain rights to make what are called data subject requests.

RetailMeNot is committed to protecting our users' privacy. To simplify the process of issuing such requests and to ensure that we respond as rapidly as possible, we've created this Privacy Portal.
