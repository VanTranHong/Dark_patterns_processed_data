Do Not Sell is a two-step process:


You do not have to be a Registered User (have an Account) to make this request.


A Do Not Sell My Personal Information (DNS) request tells 1-800-Flowers.com and its family of brands that you do not want us to sell or share  your personal information with select third parties for marketing purposes as required by the GDPR (Right to Object), California Consumer Protection Act (CCPA) and NV SB-220.


Step 1: Submit the form below with as much information as possible. The information you provide will only be used to process your request.


Step 2: Click the verification link in the email that you will receive from "1800FLWS-Brands" no-reply@trustarc.com. (If you do not receive it immediately, check your junk/spam folder, and then contact PrivacySupport@1800flowers.com.)


Once verified, the request will be completed within the timeframe permitted by applicable law (e.g., up to 30 days for EU-/UK-GDPR, 45 days for California (CCPA/CPRA), 60 days for Nevada (NV-SB220). For all other states and countries, this may take up to 90 days.

 

As permitted by applicable law, UNVERIFIED REQUESTS will not be processed.


Note: Your request is tied to the device you are using now; if you use multiple devices, you will need to complete a form from each device. Unfortunately, we cant match your device(s) to your customer record.
