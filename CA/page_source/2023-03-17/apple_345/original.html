    Now all you need is iPhone.

    With just your iPhone, you can subscribe and access over 3000 workouts and meditations. Stay motivated with onscreen coaching, timers, and trainer callouts as you work out. And track your daily fitness goals with your Move ring.

    Take it further with Apple Watch.

    See real‑time, personalized metrics onscreen, like your heart rate, the Burn Bar, and all three Activity rings. And experience the freedom of audio‑guided walks, runs, and meditations with just your watch and Bluetooth headphones.

    All the motivation
    you need.

    Fitness+ trainers are welcoming individuals, chosen as much for their incredible expertise as for their friendly approach. They don’t just create their own routines, they also appear in each other’s videos and work with Apple Music experts to create motivating playlists. It’s a true collaboration that lifts the whole team up.

    Apple Fitness+ compatibility.

    To subscribe to Apple Fitness+ all you need is iPhone 8 or later with iOS 16.1 or later or Apple Watch Series 3 or later with watchOS 7.2 or later paired with iPhone 6s or later with iOS 14.3 or later.

    You can also access Apple Fitness+ from your Apple TV with tvOS 14.3 or later and iPad with iPadOS 14.3 or later.

    To get the newest features make sure your devices are running the latest software version.

    Questions? Answers.

    • Apple Fitness+ is a fitness service available only with Apple. You can choose from a catalog of video and audio workouts and meditations led by expert trainers who also help curate music playlists for even more motivation.

    • All you need is iPhone 8 or later to subscribe to Apple Fitness+. You can also subscribe with an Apple Watch Series 3 or later paired with iPhone 6s or later. To get the newest features, make sure your devices are running the latest software version.

    • With iPhone, you can have a great Fitness+ experience with onscreen coaching, timers, and trainer callouts while also tracking your estimated Move ring progress. With Apple Watch, you also get real‑time personalized metrics onscreen, like your heart rate and the calories you burn, in every workout. And you can track the progress of all three Activity rings onscreen and see celebrations when you close them.

    • You can find Apple Fitness+ in the center tab of the Fitness app on iPhone. For Apple Watch, you can find Fitness+ Audio Workouts in the Workout app and Fitness+ Audio Meditations in the Mindfulness app. For iPad, the Fitness app is available for iPhone or Apple Watch users to download from the App Store. The Fitness app is also available on Apple TV.

    • Yes. Fitness+ is created for everyone, from beginners to advanced, with modifications to turn the level of intensity up or down in every workout. You choose the type of workout, the time, and the trainer — it’s totally up to you. There’s also a Program called Workouts for Beginners for anyone who wants to learn the basics.

All your Fitness+ workout recommendations are powered using on-device intelligence. And when you use Apple Fitness+, neither calories nor the workouts and trainers you choose will be stored along with your Apple ID.
