U.S. State Residents

When you visit our website or interact with our products and services, we use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information that might relate to you, your preferences, or your device to make the site work as you expect, for analytics, and to provide you with a more personalized web experience or targeted advertisements.

You can choose not to allow certain types of cookies, which may impact your experience on the site and the services we are able to offer to you. Review the different categories of cookies below to find out more. You may change the default settings according to your preferences. You cannot opt-out of Essential cookies, as these are deployed to ensure the proper functioning of our website. Visit our Cookie Policy to learn more.

Subject to local law, you may have certain rights regarding information related to you that we have collected from cookies. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for more information about the rights you may have.

You must be logged into Rakuten Kobo for us to save your cookie preferences. We will apply our default settings if you are not logged in.

Cookie Categories

Essential Tracking (required to use the site)

Always Enabled

We use cookies to enable essential website operations and to ensure certain features work properly, like log-in or adding products to your shopping cart. These cookies allow you to be remembered as you explore our site in a single session or if you request, from session to session.

This tracking is always enabled, otherwise, you cant view the website or shop online. They are usually only set in response to an action made by you that amounts to a request for services, such as setting your privacy preferences, logging in or filling out forms. They also assist security issues and help the site conform to regulation. You can set your browser to block or alert you about these cookies, but some parts of our site will not then work.

Examples

  • Wishlist and shopping cart functionality
  • Purchase records
  • Reading notes, highlights, bookmarks and reading statistics

Personalization

Personalization

Rakuten Kobo may collect and process information about your use of this service to subsequently personalize your reading experience or content for you in other contexts, such as personalized emails, recommendations, and personalized pages over time. Typically, the content of the site or app is used to make inferences about your interests, which inform future selection of on site or direct marketing or content.

  • You will no longer get personalized book recommendations.
  • You will no longer get personalized email notifications.

Examples

  • Personalized book recommendations (Emails and Lists)

Performance/Analytics

Performance/Analytics

We use functional tracking to analyze how our website is being used - the data helps us discover errors and develop new designs, test the effectiveness of our website, and provide insights for advertising analytics. This type of tracking allows us to count visits and traffic sources so that we can measure and improve the performance of our site. The data we gather helps us understand which pages are the most (and least) popular, and learn more about how visitors move around the site.

If you do not allow this type of tracking, we will not know when you have visited our site, may not be able to monitor its performance optimally and Kobo will no longer be able to track your website usage to improve your experience.

  • Kobo will no longer be able to track your website usage to improve your experience.

Examples

  • Click tracking
  • A/B testing
  • Product usage analytics

Third Party Advertising and Social Media Tracking

This type of tracking may be set through our site by our advertising partners. It offers you the possibility to connect you to your friends and social networks and share content from our website through social media. It may be used by those companies and social media services to show you relevant advertisements on other sites. It can track your browser across other sites and building a profile of your interests. If you do not allow these cookies, you may not be able to use or see these social media sharing tools and you will experience advertising that is not as targeted to you or your interests.

Please note that if you disable these, you will still see advertisements on our site or on other platforms; they just won't be tailored to your interests.
